    • Adjectives ending in '-ed' and '-ing'
    2019-07-17 / 1 min read

    The difference in both of them is whether describe the feels of people(some animals) or object.

    -ed

    describe emotions - they tell us how people feel.

    I was so bored in that lesson.
    He was surprised to see Helen after all those years.

    -ing

    describe the thing that cause the emotion - a boring lesson makes you feel bored.

    I can't sleep! That noise is really annoying!
    I could listen to her for hours. She's so interesting.
